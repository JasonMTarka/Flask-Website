About Me

Welcome! My name is Jason Tarka, an independent software developer and former English teacher living in Tokyo, Japan. Our modern world is full of technology which enriches our lives; IoT technology which makes the home more convenient than ever, wide-spread high-speed internet access, and video calls to keep us connected to our friends or family no matter the situation, to name a few.
In early 2020, I realized that in order to understand the modern world as it continues to evolve, I needed to learn more about the technology that is driving this unprecedented growth. Despite working 12-hour days as an English teacher in Tokyo, I began learning Python, first through online courses and then through my own projects. After having developed my own website and applications, in addition to learning MySQL and a wide range of Python modules and frameworks, I've gained a real appreciation for all that goes into creating complex programs and engaging websites.
Despite all that I have learned so far, there is still so much more I want to master. I have featured a few of my most developed projects on this site. You can see their functionality and links to their source codes by clicking the links on the above navigation bar.
I am actively searching for employment opportunities. If interested, please contact me by email (jasonmtarka@gmail.com). You can also find my LinkedIn and Github page on the right side bar.
